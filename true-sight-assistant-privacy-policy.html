What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application receives all words spoken (as recognised by Google Assistant) when using the Application. This is used to give an approriate response to questions asked by the user. This information may be stored on the server for up to 24 months.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

All information obtained by the Application is recieved via Google, please contact Google for their privacy policy regarding their used of your data before it is made available to the Application. The Application itself does not share any information with third parties.
